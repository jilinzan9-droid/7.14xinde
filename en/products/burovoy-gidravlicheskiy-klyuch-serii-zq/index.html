ZQ203-100 / ZQ203-125ZQ Series Drill Pipe Power Tongs

The ZQ series combines spinning and torque functions in an open-throat hydraulic tong for making up and breaking out drill-pipe and tool-joint connections during drilling and workover operations.

Series: ZQ203Maximum torque: 100 / 125 kN·mModels: ZQ203-100 / ZQ203-125
Request Quote on WhatsAppView Specifications

Product Overview

Integrated Spinning and Torque Control

The open-throat head can disengage from the pipe string after the connection is made up or broken out. Combining spinning and final torque in one powered assembly reduces manual handling and helps limit thread damage when the correct jaws, rollers and operating settings are used.

Operating function

The hydraulic motor and transmission rotate the pipe connection, while the tong body applies controlled make-up or break-out torque. The backup arrangement reacts the torque during operation.

Typical service

Suitable for drill-pipe and tool-joint handling in land drilling, offshore drilling and workover service when the selected model and jaw/roller set match the connection outside diameter.

Model confirmation required

ZQ203 is a series designation, not a complete ordering code. Confirm ZQ203-100 versus ZQ203-125, standard versus Type II lifting configuration, manufacturer, nameplate, serial number, jaw/roller set and hydraulic/pneumatic supply before ordering.
