852XBAKP-F6XS Hydraulic Power Take-Off

Parker Chelsea 852 Series heavy-duty, hydraulically shifted PowerShift PTO for 8-bolt transmission openings. This configuration combines pressure lubrication with an SAE C pump output for demanding oilfield and mobile hydraulic applications.

Product Overview

Heavy-duty 8-bolt PowerShift PTO for hydraulic drive systems

The Chelsea 852 Series uses a cast-iron housing, a large clutch pack and heavy-duty output-shaft ball bearings. Pressure lubrication is standard, supporting long service life in high-load mobile equipment where the transmission drives a hydraulic pump or other auxiliary equipment.

8-boltStandard transmission mounting
678 N·m500 lb-ft intermittent torque
SAE C4-bolt flange and 1-1/4 in, 14-tooth shaft
40 kgApproximate series weight

Typical applications

Oilfield equipment, bulk transporters, fire and rescue vehicles, and other heavy-duty mobile hydraulic systems requiring reliable auxiliary power from the transmission.

Configuration control

The complete model code defines the mounting, gear ratio, input gear, lubrication, shift arrangement, assembly arrangement and output interface. Quote and replacement selection must use the full code—not only “852 Series.”

Compatibility confirmation

Before ordering, confirm the transmission make and model, 8-bolt aperture, mounting orientation, vehicle voltage, hydraulic control arrangement, pump interface, required output speed and duty cycle.
