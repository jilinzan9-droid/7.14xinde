Product Overview

Eaton WCB124, WCB224, WCB324, WCB236, WCB336 and WCB436 auxiliary brakes.

Water-cooled WCB brakes are designed for braking under continuous slip and high-temperature conditions. They are pneumatically controlled, with an optional closed or open cooling system and a coolant-circulation unit. Copper friction discs provide long-term wear protection and efficient heat dissipation. WCB series brakes are divided into two types by drive method: air-cushion and piston.

Common models: WCB124, WCB224, WCB324, WCB424, WCB136, WCB236, WCB336, WCB436.
